Directions

  1. Combine all-purpose flour, powdered sugar, and salt in the bowl of a food processor. Pulse to combine. Add cold butter and pulse until the pieces are no larger than peas.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the egg yolk, vanilla, and 4 teaspoons cold water. Drizzle over the flour mixture. Pulse until the dough forms a ball. Wrap the dough in plastic, flatten into a disk, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes (or up to 3 days).
  3. Cut the chilled dough in half.

  4. Roll one half into ropes and press along the sides of the pan.
